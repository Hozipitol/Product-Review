It is bundled with 18-55 kit lens for, you cannot expect to get better results as it is for entry level photographers. I have purchased a 50 mm prime lens from amazon and it provides a crystal clear and sharp images with beautiful blur background. The camera comes with a 2 yr warranty. It also has a adjustable LCD touch screen which is great for viewing your photos. It has a mic port, hdmi port, 9 Autofocus points (which is less ofcourse) wifi and bluetooth. I have got this camera with 18-55 mm kit lens, 16 GB card and a camera bag. Its best camera for beginners who want to learn photography and it is best for taking some beautiful images...highly recommended

NIKON D-3500

Nikon 3500 it's a budget DSLR camera. The camera is not that big it is very compact and handy. Thus being budget they have not compromised in the build quality and having a solid body and speaking of the features Nikon provide a descent features for the camera. They also include a camera bag and a SSD card with the camera and a tutorial guide disk in the box packing. The pictures quality is great and the manual mode give us the ability to control the camera so you can take much better photos. the video quality is not that great which I don't consider. All together the best camera for a beginner for this price point.

SONY ILCE-6600M

The Sony a6600 looks an excellent camera for video and vlogging, and its stunning AF, in-body image stabilization and large battery make it suitable for demanding stills photography. However, its still image quality is good rather than class-leading, which is a significant shortcoming given the a6600 is up against some terrific and more keenly-priced rival cameras, and while its video performance is broadly good, its marred by pronounced rolling shutter effect and pretty ordinary video specifications by today's standards.

By: William

OLYMPUS DE-M10

The Olympus OM-D E-M10 Mark IV is the entry-level model in the company's DSLR-style lineup of Micro Four Thirds cameras (the PEN models, such as the E-PL10, are rangefinder-style). It's a step up from the beginner-focused E-PL series, offering more controls, better build quality and a broader feature set. It sports a 20MP Four Thirds sensor, in-body image stabilization, flip-down touchscreen with a simple interface and 4K video capture.

By: James
